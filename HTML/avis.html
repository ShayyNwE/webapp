One of the major challenges was the management of fluctuating schedules. Last-minute changes sometimes required us to adjust our schedules in record time. One of Dublin's major strengths lies in its well-developed transportation system, facilitated by numerous bus routes throughout the city. The bus map and its subscription are easy to obtain, available in any supermarket across the city. Additionally, students can benefit from a student card to enjoy reduced fares on bus journeys.
